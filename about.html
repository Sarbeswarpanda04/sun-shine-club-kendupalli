About Sun Shine Club

Founded by residents of Kendupalli, Sun Shine Club is a volunteer‑led community organization focused on culture, education, and welfare. We bring people together across ages and backgrounds.

From festive decorations to study support, we plan and run programs that strengthen bonds and celebrate tradition with respect and joy.

Transparency and inclusivity guide our decisions. We publish annual reports and welcome ideas from all community members.

Everyone is invited to participate — as a volunteer, donor, artist, or learner.
